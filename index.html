About

Good Time Bear is a Los Angeles-based independent production company and filmmaker collective that has produced 2 short films, with one currently in production. Our diverse crew of experienced, creative, and passionate filmmakers are all available to hire for your next production.

Good Time Bear's team has experience working different roles on dozens of productions, including award-winning short films, independent features, and national commercials broadcast on network television. Please contact us using the above form to get in touch.

    Hello! I'm Cole Davis, one of Good Time Bear's many cast and crew members available to work on your next production. I graduated from Santa Monica College's film program in 2022 and I am currently continuing my film studies at California State University, Northridge.

    In the 2 years since I moved to Los Angeles from Gilroy, California, I have done production work on 2 independent feature films, 4 independent short films, 8 music videos, and a national commercial for a prominent retail brand. During this time, I also founded Good Time Bear Productions, where I produced 2 additional independent short films, with one in production. My skills and experience are at your disposal, so please do not hesitate to reach out if you are interested in adding me to your crew.
